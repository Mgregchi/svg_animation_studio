
# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our standards

**Encouraged:**
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback on scenes and docs
- Helping newcomers get their first PR merged
- Sharing what you've built — even rough drafts

**Not acceptable:**
- Dismissive or demeaning feedback ("this is bad", "why would anyone want this")
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' personal information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [@Mgregchi](https://github.com/Mgregchi). All reports will be handled with discretion.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
